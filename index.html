Capital Ring Place Impressions

This exercise in data gathering, processing, and visualisation seeks to capture the essence of each place, revealing its unique stories and experiences. Explore the points along the Capital Ring and find inspiration for your next urban hike!

What is Capital Ring

Capital Ring is an urban walk around London. Opened in 2005, it features 78 miles (126km) of open space, nature reserves, Sites of Specific Scientific Interest and more. This scenic journey encompasses some of the city's most cherished green spaces, and provides a unique perspective on London's natural landscapes amidst its urban expanse.

Where the data comes from

Place names and locations are derived from Ordnance Survey. Place impressions are pre-generated by ChatGPT, a large language model trained by OpenAI, using batch API requests. Built areas under place labels are derived from OpenStreetMap. See the process in the Github repo.
